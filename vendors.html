Prawn Culture – Quick Guides

Pond Preparation (7–10 days)
  • Dry pond bed; apply lime as per soil pH (6.5–8.5 target).
  • Fill water 3–4 ft; chlorinate if needed; dechlorinate after 48 hrs.
  • Probiotics pre-stock; check salinity, alkalinity, hardness.
Stocking (Vannamei SPF PL)
  • Acclimatize PL (temp/salinity) for 45–60 min.
  • Density per local practice (e.g., 40–60/m² intensive).
  • Start feed @ 4–5% biomass; adjust by tray observations.
Daily Water Quality
  • DO > 5 mg/L (night aeration 8pm–3am if shallow ponds).
  • pH 7.5–8.2 (morning/evening checks).
  • Transparency 25–35 cm; color stable (golden/brown).
Health & Disease
  • Vibriosis prevention: regular probiotics, avoid overfeeding.
  • Lab checks weekly—histology if stress signs.
  • Biosecurity: bird fencing, foot dips, gear sanitize.
Harvest & Post-Harvest
  • Plan ice & van logistics (use Harvest tab calculators).
  • Use clean ice; rapid chilling; avoid freshwater wash for saline species.
